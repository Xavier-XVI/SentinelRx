# SentinelRx — Brand Ingredient Map

Use this file to identify active ingredients in common OTC combination products when a caregiver submits a brand name without a full label, or when cross-checking total daily dose accumulation.

Last verified: 2026. Formulas subject to manufacturer change — verify against current packaging for clinical decisions.

## Common Supplements

| Brand / Supplement | Active Ingredients | Typical Dose | Key Risk Flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fish Oil (standard) | Omega-3 (EPA + DHA) | 1000–1200mg | 🚫 With anticoagulants at this dose |
| Fish Oil (high-dose) | Omega-3 (EPA + DHA) | 2000–4000mg | 🚫 Major bleeding risk with anticoagulants |
| Turmeric / Curcumin | Curcumin extract | 500–1000mg | 🚫 Anticoagulant effect with warfarin/NOACs |
| Ginkgo Biloba | Ginkgo leaf extract | 120mg | 🚫 Anticoagulant + serotonin effect |
| Garlic Extract | Allicin / garlic concentrate | varies | 🚫 With anticoagulants at high dose |
| St. John's Wort | Hypericum extract | 300mg | 🚫 Multiple severe interactions — always DO NOT TAKE |
| Vitamin E | Alpha-tocopherol | 400–1000 IU | ⚠️ >400 IU: anticoagulant potentiation with warfarin |
| Vitamin D3 | Cholecalciferol | 1000–2000 IU | ✅ Generally safe; routine supplementation |
| Vitamin C | Ascorbic acid | 500–1000mg | ✅ Generally safe |
| Magnesium | Magnesium oxide/glycinate | 200–400mg | ⚠️ Check renal function; high doses affect digoxin absorption |
| Melatonin | Melatonin | 0.5–10mg | ⚠️ See sleep aids above |
| Zinc | Zinc gluconate/oxide | 10–50mg | ⚠️ High doses (>40mg) reduce antibiotic and other drug absorption |
| CoQ10 | Coenzyme Q10 | 100–400mg | ⚠️ May slightly reduce warfarin effectiveness — monitor INR |
